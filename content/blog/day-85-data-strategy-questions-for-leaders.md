## The Six Pillars

1. **Data Governance:** Standards, quality, ownership, and regulatory compliance.

2. **Data Architecture:** Defining how data flows, where it lives, and how it&apos;s modeled.

3. **Data Infrastructure:** Moving towards a Hybrid Model (on-premise and cloud) for flexibility and scale.

4. **Data Literacy &amp; Culture:** Ensuring the organization can actually use the data effectively.

5. **People &amp; Process:** Our operating model and crucial &apos;make vs. buy&apos; decisions.

6. **Success KPIs:** Defining what &apos;winning&apos; looks like.
